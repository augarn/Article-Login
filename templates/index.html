Our purpose

"The purpose of this project is to reduce food waste in the restaurant industry by improving communication between producers and consumers via our platform. Through this project, the hopes that from an environmental and socio-economic perspective can have a positive impact. The aim is also for the developers to be able to participate in building a platform from the ground up in order to obtain new knowledge and techniques. The developers should be able to work in groups and together come up with solutions that are for the project ahead."
